To My Dearest Sila

My dearest Sila,


I was thinking about you the whole time. I was thinking about every single moment we spent together, and I just realized I never showed my soul to someone, I never been known. Till I met you.


I just want you to know that you are so precious to me. Even if you are not here at the moment, you've been in my head 24/7. I sleep on the thought of you, I ask myself if you are fine, if you are healthy, if your exams were fine. You are always with me, I carry you in my mind and heart.


I trust you with my life and death and I'll keep trusting you forever. I'm more than grateful to have you, and the most important thing is that I love you the most.


I want you to know that no matter what happens, my door will always be expecting you to knock on it. You will be always welcome and loved by me, and I'll be so ready to give out my heart if it means that it will make you happy.


Like the characters in the image, I imagine us together, sharing quiet moments, finding comfort in each other's presence. Our own little world where we can just be.


Every single day, you make me feel like I'm the luckiest person. Your kindness, your smile, your voice - everything about you brings so much joy to my life.

With all my love,
Anne
